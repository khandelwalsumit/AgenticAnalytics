# ATT Rewards & Loyalty Friction Analysis Report

Analysis of 16 calls related to Rewards & Loyalty for ATT customers reveals a single, highly impactful theme. This theme is 100% preventable and is primarily driven by digital friction, with significant contributions from operations, communication, and policy gaps. The top three issues by call volume are redemption preference confusion: 7 calls, 43.75%; incorrect category bonus application: 4 calls, 25.0%; and welcome bonus discrepancies: 2 calls, 12.5%. The dominant driver for these issues is digital friction, with operations, communication, and policy also contributing. A total of 12 quick wins were identified.

### Digital
- Develop a clear and easily discoverable section in the digital experience (mobile app and web) where customers can view and modify their rewards redemption preferences (e.g., points vs. cashback).
- Enhance transparency around merchant category codes and their mapping to bonus categories within the digital experience.
- Improve the visibility and tracking of promotional offer progress and terms within the digital experience.
- Implement proactive communication and visibility for upcoming tier status changes within the digital experience.
- Provide visibility into expected and pending bonus point accruals within the digital experience.
- Implement a digital tracking mechanism for points transfers to external partners.

### Operations
- Develop and deploy a user-friendly self-service interface within the mobile app and web portal for customers to easily manage and update their rewards redemption preferences.
- Enhance the rewards system to provide clear visibility into how merchant categories are mapped to bonus earning categories, and offer a mechanism for customers to query or dispute MCC assignments.
- Implement a robust audit and reconciliation process for promotional bonus crediting to ensure that all qualifying customers receive the correct bonus amount as per offer terms.
- Establish a clear communication protocol for tier changes, including sending proactive notifications (e.g., email, push notifications) to customers well in advance of any downgrade, explaining the reasons and impact.
- Automate the process for crediting anniversary bonus points to ensure they are posted to customer accounts within a defined and communicated service level agreement (SLA).
- Review and optimize the integration and data exchange processes with airline partners to ensure points transfers are processed and reflected in partner accounts within a clear and competitive SLA.

### Communication
- Move cashback redemption preference setting to a prominent, easily accessible location (e.g., top-level rewards settings). Add a 'Set as default' option and allow customers to set auto-redemption rules (e.g., auto statement credit every 5,000 points).
- Display the MCC code and category assigned to each transaction. Allow customers to flag incorrect merchant categorization. Clearly list category bonus exclusions with examples on the rewards earning rules page.
- Add a real-time welcome bonus spend tracker on the rewards dashboard with a progress bar and days remaining. List qualifying and non-qualifying purchase categories clearly with specific merchant examples.
- Show a tier progress bar with the exact spend needed for retention/upgrade. Send 90-day and 30-day tier expiry warnings. List all tier benefits clearly with real-time access status (active/at risk).
- Display the anniversary bonus posting timeline and account anniversary date prominently in the rewards section. Send a push notification when the anniversary bonus is credited or if there's a delay.
- Show a merchant earning rate preview on each transaction. Display bonus category progress on the main rewards dashboard. Add a real-time earn rate indicator on transaction detail screens with a qualifying merchant badge.
- Show estimated partner transfer completion time and partner program confirmation. Add a transfer tracker. Display a clear irreversibility warning with partner program terms before confirming the transfer.

### Policy
- Review the internal rule that dictates the default redemption method and the accessibility of changing this preference.
- Review the internal policy on how merchant category codes are mapped to bonus categories and the transparency provided to customers.
- Review the internal rules and compliance requirements for promotional offer crediting and communication of terms.
- Review the internal policy regarding tier evaluation, notification protocols for status changes, and communication of benefit changes.
- Review the internal policy and process for crediting anniversary bonus points.
- Review the internal policy and operational procedures for points transfers to airline partners.

### Quick Wins
- Develop a clear and easily discoverable section in the digital experience (mobile app and web) where customers can view and modify their rewards redemption preferences (e.g., points vs. cashback).
- Implement an 'Offer Tracker' in the app/web dashboard showing a progress bar towards meeting spend requirements, a clear checklist of all qualifying criteria, and an estimated bonus posting date.
- Provide 60-day advance warning notifications (in-app, email, push) for impending tier downgrades. Display current tier status, qualification progress, and a comparison of benefits for different tiers in the app.
- Add an 'Expected Bonuses' section to the rewards dashboard, listing upcoming anniversary bonuses with their estimated posting dates.
- Add a 'Points Transfer Tracker' in the rewards section of the app/web, showing the status (e.g., 'Pending', 'Completed'), estimated arrival time, and partner account details for all initiated transfers.
- Develop and integrate a system that automatically triggers pre-downgrade notifications based on defined tier evaluation periods and customer activity, ensuring timely communication.
- Implement a scheduled job or real-time trigger in the rewards system to automatically credit anniversary bonus points on the account anniversary date, with monitoring for successful execution.
- Move cashback redemption preference setting to a prominent, easily accessible location (e.g., top-level rewards settings). Add a 'Set as default' option and allow customers to set auto-redemption rules (e.g., auto statement credit every 5,000 points).
