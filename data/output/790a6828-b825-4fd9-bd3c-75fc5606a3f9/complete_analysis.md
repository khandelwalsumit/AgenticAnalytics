# 16 Calls Expose a Loyalty Program Undermined by Communication Failures

An analysis of 16 customer calls about the ATT 'Rewards & Loyalty' program reveals that every single issue is rooted in poor communication, creating a clear and immediate opportunity to improve customer experience and deflect 100% of this call volume.

---

<!-- SLIDE: executive_summary | layout: callout_stat | title: "The Situation" -->

## The Situation

This report analyzes a focused set of 16 customer support calls filtered for the 'Rewards & Loyalty' call reason for 'ATT' products. The findings represent a deep dive into the specific friction points customers are experiencing with the loyalty program.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 1: Cashback Preference Confusion Drives 44% of Calls" -->

## Pain Point 1: Cashback Preference Confusion Drives 44% of Calls

**What's happening:** Customers are frustrated when their earned cashback is automatically converted to points instead of a statement credit, which they expected.
**The evidence:** Callers explicitly state they cannot find any setting or option within the app or website to change their redemption preference.
**Call volume:** 7 calls | 43.75% of total
**The fix:** Implement a prominent setting in the rewards dashboard to allow customers to easily select and modify their cashback redemption preference.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 2: Merchant Coding Issues Invalidate Bonuses, Surprising Customers" -->

## Pain Point 2: Merchant Coding Issues Invalidate Bonuses, Surprising Customers

**What's happening:** Customers are not receiving category-specific bonuses because the merchant's payment system is coded in a way that doesn't match the bonus criteria.
**The evidence:** 3 customers called because gas station purchases were coded as 'convenience stores', making them ineligible for a gas category bonus.
**Call volume:** 3 calls | 18.75% of total
**The fix:** Enhance the in-app transaction details to show the merchant category code (MCC) and the specific earn rate applied to that purchase.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 3: Partial Welcome Bonuses Erode Trust from Day One" -->

## Pain Point 3: Partial Welcome Bonuses Erode Trust from Day One

**What's happening:** New customers are meeting the spend requirements for a welcome bonus but are receiving a lower amount than advertised, with no explanation.
**The evidence:** 2 customers called after receiving 40,000 points instead of the expected 60,000, causing immediate confusion and distrust.
**Call volume:** 2 calls | 12.5% of total
**The fix:** Automate a detailed notification upon bonus credit that explains the amount received and directly references the offer terms fulfilled.

## Rewards & Loyalty: The Story

Customers believe they are earning and redeeming rewards correctly, only to find their cashback is in the wrong format, bonuses are missing, or their tier status has changed without warning. These communication gaps create frustrating, high-effort experiences and drive 100% of the loyalty-related calls analyzed, turning a program meant to build loyalty into a source of friction.
