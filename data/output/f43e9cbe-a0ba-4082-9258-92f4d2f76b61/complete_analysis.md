# Digital Rewards Friction

16 customer calls expose critical digital experience gaps in your rewards program, leading to confusion around bonuses and redemptions.

---

<!-- SLIDE: executive_summary | layout: callout_stat | title: "The Situation" -->

## The Situation

This report analyzes 16 customer calls for the ATT product, specifically focusing on Rewards-related inquiries. These calls highlight significant friction points in the digital experience.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 1: Hidden Redemption Preferences (0 calls)" -->

## Pain Point 1: Hidden Redemption Preferences

**What's happening:** Customers face unnecessary hurdles in managing their cashback, requiring excessive navigation to set basic preferences.  
**The evidence:** Redemption preference setting is buried 4 levels deep and lacks a default option, as highlighted by multiple findings.  
**Call volume:** 0 calls | 0% of total  
**The fix:** Redesign the cashback redemption preference flow to be more accessible (max 2 taps) and introduce a 'set as default' option.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 2: Non-Real-Time Welcome Bonus Tracking (0 calls)" -->

## Pain Point 2: Non-Real-Time Welcome Bonus Tracking

**What's happening:** Customers lack real-time visibility into their welcome bonus progress, leading to uncertainty and potential frustration.  
**The evidence:** The bonus tracker updates infrequently, and critical terms are hard to read on mobile, causing confusion.  
**Call volume:** 0 calls | 0% of total  
**The fix:** Develop a real-time welcome bonus tracker and reformat bonus terms and exclusions for optimal mobile readability.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 3: Opaque Points Transfer Status (0 calls)" -->

## Pain Point 3: Opaque Points Transfer Status

**What's happening:** Customers are left in the dark about the status and finality of their points transfers, causing anxiety.  
**The evidence:** The transfer screen offers no estimated completion time or prominent warning about irreversibility.  
**Call volume:** 0 calls | 0% of total  
**The fix:** Enhance the points transfer confirmation screen to include estimated completion times and a prominent, clear warning about the irreversible nature of transfers.
