# 16 Calls Expose Critical Rewards & Loyalty Failures Costing Avoidable Contacts Monthly

16 customer calls expose a critical self-service failure in ATT Rewards & Loyalty, concentrated in digital experience gaps and communication failures, costing your team avoidable contacts every month.

---

<!-- SLIDE: executive_summary | layout: callout_stat | title: "The Situation" -->

## The Situation

This report analyzes 16 customer calls specifically concerning ATT Rewards & Loyalty, revealing a single, highly preventable theme. The analysis focused on calls where the primary reason was related to rewards and loyalty programs for the ATT product.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 1: Partner Transfer Delays Drive 4 Calls (25.0%)" -->

## Pain Point 1: Partner Transfer Delays Drive 4 Calls (25.0%)

**What's happening:** Customers lack visibility into the status and expected timelines of partner point transfers, leading to confusion and calls.
**The evidence:** 4 customers called explicitly about delays in their partner point transfers, indicating a clear gap in proactive communication and tracking.
**Call volume:** 4 calls | 25.0% of total
**The fix:** Implement a real-time transfer status tracker in the mobile app and web, with estimated completion times and push notifications for status changes.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 2: Promotional Points Missing Drives 3 Calls (18.75%)" -->

## Pain Point 2: Promotional Points Missing Drives 3 Calls (18.75%)

**What's happening:** Customers are not receiving promotional points as expected, likely due to a lack of clarity around eligibility criteria or an inability to track offer progress.
**The evidence:** 3 customers called because promotional points they anticipated were not credited to their account.
**Call volume:** 3 calls | 18.75% of total
**The fix:** Develop an in-app offer tracker with progress bars and clear, concise qualifying criteria for each promotion.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 3: Points Not Credited Drives 3 Calls (18.75%)" -->

## Pain Point 3: Points Not Credited Drives 3 Calls (18.75%)

**What's happening:** Points from qualifying transactions are not appearing in customer balances, suggesting a lack of transparency regarding points posting and earn rates.
**The evidence:** 3 customers initiated calls because points from their transactions were absent from their rewards balance.
**Call volume:** 3 calls | 18.75% of total
**The fix:** Add a 'pending points' section to the rewards dashboard and display the earn rate per transaction in the transaction history.
