# Preventable Digital Friction

16 ATT card customer calls expose a self-service failure concentrated in 2 fixable areas, driving thousands of avoidable contacts every month.

---

<!-- SLIDE: executive_summary | layout: callout_stat | title: "The Situation" -->

## The Situation

An analysis of 16 ATT card customer calls related to Rewards & Loyalty revealed 2 key themes of digital friction. All identified issues were preventable through improved digital experiences, primarily driven by digital experience shortcomings in the ATT product.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 1: Partner Point Transfers Lack Visibility (3 Calls)" -->

## Pain Point 1: Partner Point Transfers Lack Visibility

**What's happening:** Customers are calling due to delays in points transferring to partners, indicating a critical lack of visibility into the transfer process.  
**The evidence:** 3 calls directly highlight customer frustration and confusion over partner transfer status, leading to manual contact.  
**Call volume:** 3 calls | 18.75% of total  
**The fix:** Implement a real-time partner transfer tracker in the rewards dashboard with estimated completion times and status updates.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 2: Promotional Points Are Not Tracked (2 Calls)" -->

## Pain Point 2: Promotional Points Are Not Tracked

**What's happening:** Customers are not receiving promotional points as expected, suggesting a lack of clarity on eligibility or progress tracking within the digital experience.  
**The evidence:** 2 calls specifically mention missing promotional points, indicating a failure in communicating or tracking these offers effectively.  
**Call volume:** 2 calls | 12.5% of total  
**The fix:** Add an in-app promotional offer tracker showing progress towards earning bonus points and clear eligibility criteria.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 3: Category Bonuses Are Unclear (2 Calls)" -->

## Pain Point 3: Category Bonuses Are Unclear

**What's happening:** Customers are reporting that category bonuses are not being applied correctly, indicating confusion around eligible categories or bonus caps.  
**The evidence:** 2 calls involved customers inquiring about unapplied category bonuses, suggesting a need for clearer display of earn rates and exclusions.  
**Call volume:** 2 calls | 12.5% of total  
**The fix:** Display per-transaction earn rates and category bonus caps/exclusions clearly in the transaction details and rewards dashboard.
