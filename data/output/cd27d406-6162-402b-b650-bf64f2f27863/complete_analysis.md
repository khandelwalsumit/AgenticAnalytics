<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 1: Welcome Bonus Tracker Lacks Real-time Updates" -->

## Pain Point 1: Welcome Bonus Tracker Lacks Real-time Updates

**What's happening:** Customers are experiencing significant frustration due to a welcome bonus tracker that does not provide real-time updates on their progress, leading to uncertainty and repeated contacts. Additionally, bonus terms are difficult to access and read on mobile devices.
**The evidence:** The tracker updates only once per statement cycle, creating a lag in information. Bonus terms and qualifying purchase exclusions are poorly formatted for mobile viewing.
**Call volume:** 0 calls | 0% of total
**The fix:** Develop a real-time welcome bonus tracker with a visual progress bar and redesign bonus terms for mobile readability.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 2: Hidden Cashback Redemption Settings Cause Confusion" -->

## Pain Point 2: Hidden Cashback Redemption Settings Cause Confusion

**What's happening:** Customers struggle to find and manage their cashback redemption preferences because the setting is buried deep within the application's navigation, requiring multiple taps to access.
**The evidence:** The cashback redemption preference setting is hidden four levels deep (Rewards > Settings > Preferences > Redemption Method), and there is no option to set a default redemption method.
**Call volume:** 0 calls | 0% of total
**The fix:** Simplify the navigation path for cashback redemption preferences to 1-2 taps and introduce a feature allowing users to set a default redemption method.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 3: Opaque MCC and Bonus Exclusions Fuel Calls" -->

## Pain Point 3: Opaque MCC and Bonus Exclusions Fuel Calls

**What's happening:** Customers are confused and frustrated by the lack of transparency regarding Merchant Category Codes (MCC) used for bonus determination and unclear category bonus exclusions.
**The evidence:** The MCC for transactions is not visible, and explanations for category bonus exclusions are hard to find and understand.
**Call volume:** 0 calls | 0% of total
**The fix:** Display the MCC for each transaction within the app and create an easily accessible section explaining category bonus exclusions with examples.

## Rewards: The Story

Customers are frequently calling about various rewards-related issues, indicating a significant lack of clarity and functionality within the digital rewards experience. This fragmentation forces customers to seek agent assistance for basic information and preference management, leading to unnecessary contact volume and eroding trust in the rewards program.

#### Core Issue

The digital rewards experience is fragmented and lacks transparency, forcing customers to call for basic information or to manage their preferences, rather than self-serving within the application.

#### Primary Driver

The welcome bonus tracker not being real-time is a significant point of frustration, contributing to calls due to customer uncertainty about bonus eligibility and progress.

#### Solutions

1.  Develop a real-time welcome bonus tracker `[Digital]`
2.  Simplify cashback redemption path `[Digital]`
3.  Display MCC for transactions and explain exclusions `[Digital]`
