# 16 Calls, 1 Theme: Fixing Digital Gaps Can Deflect 100% of Loyalty Friction

Analysis of 16 customer calls reveals that all loyalty-related friction stems from a single theme—Rewards & Loyalty—driven by fixable digital visibility gaps and communication failures that are causing repeat, preventable contacts.

---

<!-- SLIDE: executive_summary | layout: callout_stat | title: "The Situation" -->

## The Situation

This analysis covers 16 customer calls filtered for the "Rewards & Loyalty" call reason for the "ATT" product. The findings point to a single, critical theme around program friction, with 90% of the volume deemed preventable through improved digital experiences and proactive communication.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 1: Partner Transfer Delays Drive 25% of Calls" -->

## Pain Point 1: Partner Transfer Delays Drive 25% of Calls

**What's happening:** Customers are calling because point transfers from partners are not appearing in their accounts within the expected timeframe, causing confusion and concern.
**The evidence:** Customers have no visibility into the transfer status after initiating it, leading them to call for manual confirmation.
**Call volume:** 4 calls | 25% of total
**The fix:** Implement a digital tracker for partner transfers showing status, estimated completion, and sending a push notification upon completion.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 2: Missing Promotional Points Confuse Customers" -->

## Pain Point 2: Missing Promotional Points Confuse Customers

**What's happening:** Customers are not receiving expected bonus points from promotions and are calling to understand why they were not eligible or why the credit failed.
**The evidence:** The lack of an in-app progress tracker for promotional offers leaves customers uncertain about their status and qualification.
**Call volume:** 3 calls | 18.75% of total
**The fix:** Develop an in-app promotional offer tracker with clear progress bars, eligibility criteria, and proactive notifications.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 3: Standard Points Credits Are Untraceable" -->

## Pain Point 3: Standard Points Credits Are Untraceable

**What's happening:** Customers' standard points from regular transactions are not appearing as expected, leading to calls questioning the loyalty program's reliability.
**The evidence:** The rewards dashboard lacks a "pending points" view, leaving customers unable to confirm if their earnings are in the process of being credited.
**Call volume:** 3 calls | 18.75% of total
**The fix:** Add a "pending points" view to the rewards dashboard that displays earn rates per transaction and estimated posting dates.

## Rewards & Loyalty: The Story

The entire volume of analyzed calls falls under this single theme. Customers are engaging with the loyalty program but are forced to call when digital tools fail to provide basic visibility into points transfers, promotional bonuses, and standard credits. This friction undermines program trust and creates unnecessary operational load from entirely preventable inquiries.
