# 16 Calls. A Single Root Cause. Digital Solutions to Fix.

16 customer calls expose a self-service failure concentrated in Rewards & Loyalty, driven by a lack of visibility and delayed information, costing your team avoidable contacts every month.

---

<!-- SLIDE: executive_summary | layout: callout_stat | title: "The Situation" -->

## The Situation

This report analyzes 16 customer calls related to Rewards and Loyalty programs for the ATT product. These calls reveal critical friction points where customers are unable to self-serve, primarily due to insufficient digital tools and information.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 1: Missing Points and Lack of Tracking Generate 6 Calls" -->

## Pain Point 1: Missing Points and Lack of Tracking

**What's happening:** Customers are not seeing promotional points or regular points credited to their account and lack visibility into pending points or promotion progress. This forces manual inquiries.
**The evidence:** The primary driver "Lack of visibility/tracking for points (pending, not credited, promotional)" accounts for 6 calls.
**Call volume:** 6 calls | 37.5% of total
**The fix:** Implement a 'pending points' section on the rewards dashboard and a promotional offer tracker.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 2: Partner Transfer Delays Drive 4 Calls" -->

## Pain Point 2: Partner Transfer Delays Drive 4 Calls

**What's happening:** Customers are calling due to delays in partner rewards transfers and a complete lack of visibility into the transfer status, leading to uncertainty and frustration.
**The evidence:** The driver "Partner Transfer Delay (lack of tracking/visibility)" accounts for 4 calls.
**Call volume:** 4 calls | 25.0% of total
**The fix:** Implement a real-time transfer tracker in the app/web with status and estimated completion.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 3: Unclear Redemption Minimums Cause 3 Calls" -->

## Pain Point 3: Unclear Redemption Minimums Cause 3 Calls

**What's happening:** Customers are frustrated by high redemption minimums that are not clearly communicated before they attempt to redeem, leading to failed self-service attempts and calls.
**The evidence:** The driver "Redemption Minimum Too High (lack of eligibility visibility)" accounts for 3 calls.
**Call volume:** 3 calls | 18.75% of total
**The fix:** Prominently display minimum redemption thresholds on the rewards dashboard and within the redemption flow.

## System Bug/Outage: The Story

Customers are experiencing significant friction due to system-related issues that directly impact their rewards and loyalty experience, leading to confusion and a lack of trust. The absence of real-time information forces customers to call, escalating simple inquiries into avoidable contacts.

#### Core Issue

The core issue is a lack of transparency and real-time status updates within the digital platform for critical rewards processes like partner transfers, promotional point crediting, and general point posting, leading to customer calls when expected outcomes don't materialize.

#### Primary Driver

The primary driver is "Partner Transfer Delay (digital, 3 calls)", where customers lack visibility into the status and estimated completion of their points transfers to partners, leading to 3 calls for updates.

#### Solutions

1.  Implement a real-time transfer status tracker in the app for partner points. `[Digital]`
2.  Add an in-app promotional offer tracker with progress bar and clear eligibility criteria. `[Digital]`
3.  Display 'pending points' view in the rewards dashboard with estimated posting dates. `[Digital]`

## Other: The Story

While categorized as "Other," these 4 calls still point to critical gaps in the digital self-service experience for rewards. Customers are seeking basic information or resolution for issues that could be easily managed with improved digital tools, indicating systemic friction.

#### Core Issue

The core issue across these miscellaneous calls is a consistent lack of digital tools for customers to track, understand, and manage their rewards points, leading to calls for issues like redemption minimums, bonus application, transfer delays, and uncredited points.

#### Primary Driver

The primary drivers are a collection of individual issues, each accounting for 1 call: Redemption Minimum Too High, Category Bonus Not Applied, Partner Transfer Delay, and Points Not Credited. These individually highlight a lack of digital self-service options.

#### Solutions

1.  Clearly display redemption minimums and options for partial redemption or alternatives. `[Digital]`
2.  Implement an in-app bonus tracker or detailed transaction view showing how points were calculated. `[Digital]`
3.  Add a 'Transfer History' section with real-time status updates and estimated completion times. `[Digital]`
