## The Situation

This report analyzes 16 customer calls related to Rewards & Loyalty for ATT customers. These calls were specifically filtered for issues within the Rewards & Loyalty domain, revealing critical areas for immediate improvement.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 1: Partner Transfer Status Generates 1 in 4 Calls (4 Calls)" -->

## Pain Point 1: Partner Transfer Status Generates 1 in 4 Calls

**What's happening:** Customers are unable to track the status or expected timeline of their points transferred to partners, leading to calls for updates and clarification.  
**The evidence:** 4 calls were logged due to a lack of clear visibility and communication regarding partner transfer status and timelines.  
**Call volume:** 4 calls | 25.0% of total  
**The fix:** Implement a real-time partner transfer status tracker in the mobile app and web portal, with proactive notifications.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 2: Missing Promotional Points Drive 3 Calls (3 Calls)" -->

## Pain Point 2: Missing Promotional Points Drive 3 Calls

**What's happening:** Customers are not receiving promotional points as expected, often due to unclear terms, poor eligibility communication, or absent crediting notifications.  
**The evidence:** 3 calls highlight a pattern of missing promotional points, indicating a breakdown in offer tracking, clear eligibility, and proactive communication.  
**Call volume:** 3 calls | 18.75% of total  
**The fix:** Develop an in-app promotional offer tracker with clear eligibility criteria, progress bars, and push notifications.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 3: Lack of Pending Points Visibility Drives 3 Calls (3 Calls)" -->

## Pain Point 3: Lack of Pending Points Visibility Drives 3 Calls

**What's happening:** Points are not credited as expected, and customers lack visibility into pending points or per-transaction earn rates, forcing them to call for clarification.  
**The evidence:** 3 calls explicitly cite points not being credited as expected, coupled with insufficient visibility into pending points or posting timelines.  
**Call volume:** 3 calls | 18.75% of total  
**The fix:** Display pending points and per-transaction earn rates in the rewards dashboard and transaction history, with notifications upon crediting.
