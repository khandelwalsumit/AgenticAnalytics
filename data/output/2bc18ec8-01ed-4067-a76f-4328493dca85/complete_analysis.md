# 16 Calls. 3 Digital Root Causes. 1 Quarter to Fix Rewards Friction.

16 customer calls expose a critical self-service failure in Rewards & Loyalty, concentrated in 3 fixable digital areas costing your team avoidable contacts every month.

---

<!-- SLIDE: executive_summary | layout: callout_stat | title: "The Situation" -->

## The Situation

This report analyzes 16 customer calls specifically filtered for "Rewards & Loyalty" call reasons related to "ATT" products. The analysis reveals that 100% of these calls are preventable, driven by digital friction, and present a significant opportunity for immediate improvement.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 1: Partner Transfer Delays Generate 1 in 4 Calls" -->

## Pain Point 1: Partner Transfer Delays Generate 1 in 4 Calls

**What's happening:** Customers are calling due to significant delays in partner points transfers and a complete lack of visibility into the transfer status. This creates uncertainty and drives avoidable live agent contact.
**The evidence:** 4 calls explicitly cited frustrations with unknown transfer timelines and no self-service tracking.
**Call volume:** 4 calls | 25.0% of total
**The fix:** Implement a real-time partner transfer status tracker in the app/web, providing estimated completion times and proactive push notifications for status changes.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 2: Missing Promotional Points Drives 18.75% of Calls" -->

<h2>Pain Point 2: Missing Promotional Points Drives 18.75% of Calls</h2>

**What's happening:** Customers are not receiving promotional points as expected and lack a digital way to track offer progress or understand eligibility criteria, leading to confusion and calls.
**The evidence:** 3 calls were directly related to promotional points not being credited, with customers seeking clarification and resolution.
**Call volume:** 3 calls | 18.75% of total
**The fix:** Develop an in-app/web offer tracker with a progress bar, clearly outlining qualifying criteria, terms and conditions, and estimated bonus posting dates, with proactive notifications.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 3: Uncredited Points Fuel 18.75% of Customer Contacts" -->

<h2>Pain Point 3: Uncredited Points Fuel 18.75% of Customer Contacts</h2>

**What's happening:** Customers are calling because points are not being credited to their accounts, and they have no visibility into pending points or reasons for non-crediting.
**The evidence:** 3 calls were logged for points not appearing in customer accounts, indicating a lack of transparency in the crediting process.
**Call volume:** 3 calls | 18.75% of total
**The fix:** Introduce a 'pending points' section in the rewards dashboard, showing points from recent transactions, estimated posting dates, and clear explanations for any uncredited points.
