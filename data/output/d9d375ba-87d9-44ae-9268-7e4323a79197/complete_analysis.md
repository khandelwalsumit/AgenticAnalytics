## The Situation

This report analyzes 16 customer calls specifically related to Rewards inquiries for the ATT product. The analysis reveals significant friction points in the self-service customer journey, primarily within rewards management and visibility, indicating a clear opportunity for digital optimization.

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 1: Hidden Cashback Redemption Settings (0 Calls)" -->

## Pain Point 1: Hidden Cashback Redemption Settings

**What's happening:** Customers struggle to find and manage their cashback redemption preferences due to deeply nested navigation within the digital platform.  
**The evidence:** The setting is hidden four levels deep (Rewards > Settings > Preferences > Redemption Method) and lacks a 'Set as Default' option, leading to confusion.  
**Call volume:** 0 calls | 0% of total  
**The fix:** Simplify navigation to redemption preferences (max 2 taps) and add a 'Set as Default' option.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 2: Unclear Welcome Bonus Progress (0 Calls)" -->

## Pain Point 2: Unclear Welcome Bonus Progress

**What's happening:** Customers lack real-time visibility into their welcome bonus qualifying spend progress, causing anxiety and uncertainty.  
**The evidence:** The welcome bonus tracker updates only once per statement cycle, and bonus terms are poorly formatted for mobile readability.  
**Call volume:** 0 calls | 0% of total  
**The fix:** Implement a real-time welcome bonus spend tracker in the mobile app and optimize bonus terms for mobile readability.

---

<!-- SLIDE: pain_point | layout: three_column | title: "Pain Point 3: Missing Anniversary Bonus Information (0 Calls)" -->

## Pain Point 3: Missing Anniversary Bonus Information

**What's happening:** Customers are uninformed about their account anniversary date and the expected posting timelines for anniversary bonuses.  
**The evidence:** The anniversary bonus posting timeline is not documented, and the account anniversary date is not displayed in-app, preventing self-service.  
**Call volume:** 0 calls | 0% of total  
**The fix:** Display the account anniversary date in-app, document anniversary bonus posting timelines, and add an in-app tracker for pending bonuses.

## Rewards Management & Visibility: The Story

Customers are frequently encountering difficulties in managing their rewards, leading to confusion and frustration. The lack of clear visibility into bonus progress, redemption options, and account milestones forces them to seek assistance, generating avoidable contacts.

#### Core Issue

The primary issue is the lack of intuitive digital self-service tools and transparent information regarding rewards programs, forcing customers to call for basic inquiries that should be resolvable independently.

#### Primary Driver

The most significant driver is the poor digital experience around bonus trackers and redemption settings, contributing to 0 calls directly but creating widespread friction across all 16 calls related to rewards.

#### Solutions

1.  Simplify rewards redemption navigation `[Digital]`
2.  Implement real-time welcome bonus tracker `[Digital]`
3.  Display account anniversary and bonus timelines `[Digital]`
